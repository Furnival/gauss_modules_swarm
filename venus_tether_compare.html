Architectural verdict Comparing architectures…
A reader's instrument, not a mission design. Atmospheric density uses a piecewise-exponential approximation to the COSPAR VIRA profile. Tether dynamics are treated quasi-statically — Coriolis, libration, and tension oscillation are not modeled. Drag uses free-molecular hyperthermal coefficients at Cd ≈ 2.2 for the bus and Cd ≈ 1.6 for the scoop tip at low velocity. The chemistry classification reflects the dissociation regime at the encounter velocity: below 2 km/s, molecules survive intact; from 2 to 5 km/s, partial dissociation; above 5 km/s, substantial dissociation into C, O, CO. The lesson the interactive teaches is architectural, not numerical: the rotating tether buys chemically intact CO₂ and an order-of-magnitude lower drag tax at the cost of raw mass throughput and structural complexity. The elliptical drag-through buys raw mass and structural simplicity at the cost of hyperthermally dissociated samples. The choice depends on what your science deck needs from the gas — mass, or molecules. Built as a companion to Rocket Lab's Venus (Atmospheric) Sample Return — Lawrence Furnival, 2026.
